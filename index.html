DAY 1 — CHEST & TRICEPS

Barbell Bench Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
📸 How to do it: Lie flat on the bench, feet on floor. Grip barbell wider than shoulder-width. Lower bar to mid-chest, press up explosively. Keep shoulder blades squeezed.

Incline Dumbbell Press

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS
📸 How to do it: Set bench to 30-45° incline. Hold dumbbells at chest level. Press up until arms extended. Squeeze upper chest at top, lower slowly.

Tricep Dips

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS
📸 How to do it: Use parallel bars or bench edge. Lower body until elbows at 90°. Push back up without locking elbows. Keep body close to bars.

DAY 2 — BACK & BICEPS

Pull-Ups

4 SETS × 6-10 REPS
📸 How to do it: Grip bar with palms facing away, hands shoulder-width. Pull up until chin clears bar. Lower with control. Engage lats throughout.

Barbell Row

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
📸 How to do it: Hinge at hips with straight back. Pull barbell to lower chest. Squeeze shoulder blades together. Lower weight with control.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS
📸 How to do it: Stand with dumbbells at sides, palms forward. Curl weights up keeping elbows fixed. Squeeze biceps at top, lower slowly.
⬆ BACK TO HOME

DAY 3 — LEGS & CORE

Barbell Squat

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
📸 How to do it: Place barbell across upper back. Feet shoulder-width apart. Squat until thighs parallel to floor. Drive through heels to stand up.

Walking Lunges

3 SETS × 12 PER LEG
📸 How to do it: Step forward, lower hips until both knees at 90°. Keep front knee aligned with ankle. Push through front heel to next lunge.

Forearm Plank

3 SETS × 45-60 SECONDS
📸 How to do it: Forearms on ground, elbows under shoulders. Body in straight line. Brace core, squeeze glutes, breathe steadily.
⬆ BACK TO HOME

DAY 4 — SHOULDERS & ARMS

Overhead Shoulder Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
📸 How to do it: Hold barbell at shoulder level. Press overhead until arms extended. Lower back with control. Keep core tight.

Lateral Raises

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS
📸 How to do it: Hold dumbbells at sides, slight bend in elbows. Raise arms to sides until parallel to floor. Pause, then lower slowly.

Skull Crushers

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS
📸 How to do it: Lie on bench, hold EZ-bar above chest. Lower weight toward forehead. Extend arms back up. Keep elbows pointed to ceiling.
