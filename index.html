DAY 1 — CHEST & TRICEPS

🏋️

Barbell Bench Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Lie flat, grip wider than shoulders, lower to mid-chest, press up.

📐

Incline Barbell Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Bench at 30-45°, lower bar to upper chest, press up.

📉

Decline Barbell Press

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS

Decline bench, lower bar to lower chest, press up.

🦅

Dumbbell Flyes

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

Lie flat, arms wide, slight bend, bring dumbbells together over chest.

🔗

Cable Crossover

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

High pulleys, step forward, pull cables down and across body.

🤸

Push-Ups

3 SETS × To Failure

Hands shoulder-width, lower body to ground, push up.

⬇️

Tricep Dips

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

Parallel bars or bench, lower until 90°, push up.

🔽

Tricep Pushdowns

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

Cable with bar/rope, elbows pinned, push down.

💀

Skull Crushers

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS

EZ-bar, lower to forehead, extend elbows.

📏

Close-Grip Bench Press

3 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Hands shoulder-width, elbows tucked, press from lower chest.
