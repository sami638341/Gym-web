DAY 1 — CHEST & TRICEPS

🏋️

Bench Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Lie flat, grip bar wider than shoulders, lower to chest, press up.

📐

Incline Dumbbell Press

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS

Bench at 30-45°, press dumbbells from chest, squeeze upper chest.

⬇️

Tricep Dips

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

Use parallel bars, lower until elbows 90°, push back up.

⬆ Back to Home

DAY 2 — BACK & BICEPS

⬆️

Pull-Ups

4 SETS × 6-10 REPS

Overhand grip, pull chin above bar, lower with control.

🚣

Barbell Row

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Bend at hips, back straight, pull bar to lower chest.

💪

Dumbbell Curl

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS

Stand, curl dumbbells up, keep elbows fixed at sides.

⬆ Back to Home

DAY 3 — LEGS

🦵

Barbell Squat

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Bar on upper back, squat until thighs parallel, drive up through heels.

🏋️

Romanian Deadlift

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS

Hinge at hips, slight knee bend, lower bar along legs.

🚶

Walking Lunges

3 SETS × 12 per leg

Step forward, lower back knee, keep front knee over ankle.

⬆ Back to Home

DAY 4 — SHOULDERS

🏋️

Overhead Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Press barbell from shoulders overhead, keep core tight.

🕊️

Lateral Raises

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

Raise dumbbells to sides until parallel, slight bend in elbows.

🎯

Face Pulls

3 SETS × 15-20 REPS

Pull rope towards face, squeeze shoulder blades.

⬆ Back to Home

DAY 5 — ARMS

💪

Barbell Curl

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Curl barbell up, keep elbows stationary, squeeze at top.

💀

Skull Crushers

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS

Lower EZ-bar to forehead, extend arms, elbows pointed up.

🔨

Hammer Curl

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS

Neutral grip, curl dumbbells together, squeeze biceps.

⬆ Back to Home

DAY 6 — CORE & CARDIO

🧘

Plank

3 SETS × 45-60 sec

Forearms on ground, body straight, brace core, breathe.

🌀

Russian Twists

3 SETS × 20 per side

Sit balanced, twist torso side to side with weight.

🏃

Burpees

3 SETS × 12-15

Squat, jump back to plank, jump up explosively.

⬆ Back to Home

DAY 7 — FULL BODY

🏋️

Deadlift

4 SETS × 6-8 REPS

Hip hinge, lift bar from floor, lock out hips and knees.

📤

Push Press

3 SETS × 8-10 REPS

Dip knees slightly, drive bar overhead in one motion.

🔔

Kettlebell Swing

3 SETS × 15-20 REPS

Hinge at hips, swing kettlebell to chest height.

⬆ Back to Home
