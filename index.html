DAY 1 · CHEST & TRICEPS

Bench Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
Lie flat, grip bar, lower to chest, press up explosively.

Incline Dumbbell Press

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS
Incline bench, dumbbells at chest, press up, squeeze upper chest.

Tricep Dips

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS
Parallel bars or bench, lower body, extend arms fully.

DAY 2 · BACK & BICEPS

Pull-Ups

4 SETS × 6-10 REPS
Overhand grip, pull chin above bar, control descent.

Barbell Row

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
Bent-over, pull bar to lower chest, squeeze back.

Dumbbell Curl

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS
Stand tall, curl weights, keep elbows fixed.

DAY 3 · LEGS & CORE

Barbell Squat

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
Bar on shoulders, squat to parallel, drive through heels.

Lunges

3 SETS × 12 PER LEG
Step forward, lower back knee, keep torso upright.

Plank

3 SETS × 45-60 SEC
Forearm position, body straight, engage core.

DAY 4 · SHOULDERS & ARMS

Overhead Press

4 SETS × 8-10 REPS
Barbell/dumbbells, press overhead, lock out arms.

Lateral Raises

3 SETS × 12-15 REPS
Raise dumbbells to sides, slight bend in elbows.

Skull Crushers

3 SETS × 10-12 REPS
Lying tricep extension, lower bar to forehead, extend.
